## Accomplishments
- Badge award (any path through `awardBadge`) sends push with badge icon, title, and description; deep-links to `/kid/achievements`
- Any coin credit or debit via `updateWalletCoins` sends push with signed delta and description; deep-links to `/kid/wallet`

## Decisions Made
- Push call in `awardBadge` placed after the XP update block: badge insert + XP update both complete before push fires, so a push failure cannot cause a DB rollback or incomplete badge record.
- Single hook in `updateWalletCoins` rather than individual `awardCoinsFor*` wrappers: covers 100% of coin events with one change — grades, room, behavior, sport, P2P transfers, purchase approvals, manual adjustments.
- Signed delta format: `+N монет` for credits, `-N монет` for debits — clear at a glance in push notification body.
