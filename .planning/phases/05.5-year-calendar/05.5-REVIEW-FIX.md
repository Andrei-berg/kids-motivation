### WR-04: `applyPreset('replace')` non-atomic delete-then-insert

**Files modified:** `lib/vacation-presets.ts`
**Commit:** c2e22e3
**Applied fix:** Replace mode now inserts the fresh preset rows first
(`insert(...).select('id')`), then deletes only the old rows (family+preset
scope, `id NOT IN (newIds)`). A failed insert leaves the old rows intact; a
failed delete leaves recoverable duplicates that the next replace-apply cleans
up — never a window with the periods gone.
