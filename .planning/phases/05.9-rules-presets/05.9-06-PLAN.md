<objective>
De-hardcode the award computation: replace the numeric grade switch with a `grade_coin_map[grade]` lookup at both call sites, and replace the single `day.good_behavior` boolean with a sum over that day's `approved` behavior_marks. Resolve the numeric→string `grade` type break in badges/streaks (type-only, D-07 keeps them 5-point-only). Cover it with integration tests.

RESOLVED Open Question 1 (Pitfall 4 — day-blocks behavior seam): behavior tags credit UNIFORMLY in the flag-OFF and flag-ON paths as a sum-over-approved-marks, each mark contributing exactly its `tag.price`. NO day-type multiplier is applied per mark, and the flag-ON `behavior` day_block's visibility gate does NOT gate whether marks are creditable. Rationale: a behavior mark is an explicit signed assessment (a parent's judgement or an approved child proposal), not a schedule-derived work slot — multiplying "Rude to a parent −10" by a vacation multiplier is semantically wrong, and running both paths through one loop preserves Phase 5.6's byte-parity discipline.

Purpose: The authoritative money computation for grades and behavior.
Output: Award route + day-blocks reconciliation + service type fixes + tests.
</objective>
