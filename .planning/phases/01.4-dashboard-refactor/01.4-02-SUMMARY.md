## Decisions Made
- `members` prop on ExpenseModal made required (not optional) — an empty prop silently breaks the child picker UI more subtly than a required-prop TS error
- `displayName` props on AuditLogViewer and PotentialWidget kept optional for backward compatibility — callers not yet updated in Plan 02 can still compile
- Inline auto-fix to `app/expenses/page.tsx` preferred over making `members` optional — required prop enforces correct wiring at compile time
