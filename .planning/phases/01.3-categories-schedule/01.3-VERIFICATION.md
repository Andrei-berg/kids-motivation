## Notable Limitation: ScheduleEditor getFamilyChildren Scope

`ScheduleEditor.tsx` calls `getFamilyChildren(familyId)` from `onboarding-api.ts`. That function filters children with `.is('user_id', null)` — only returning children who have NOT yet linked an account. In production use, once a child registers and links their account, they disappear from the ScheduleEditor child tabs.

This is a functional gap: the ScheduleEditor cannot display schedule items for linked children. The `TaskManager` has the same limitation for its child assignment dropdown. This limitation is documented in the summary as a known behavior, and is flagged here for human verification.

This does NOT block Phase 1.3's primary goal (infrastructure is live), but it will need to be addressed in Phase 1.4 when the dashboard refactors to use UUID-based member selection.
