## Decisions Made
- Push notification text: used the plan's literal strings as the push **title**, with a short derived **body** (e.g. `title: 'Секция сегодня'`, `body: '{title} в {HH:MM}'`) since `sendPushToSubscription(subscriptionJson, title, body, url)` requires the two separately — the plan's `<action>` text gave a single combined string per check without specifying the title/body split.
- Kept `getStreaksAtRisk` scoped to a single child per call (loops over child members in the route) rather than batching all children into one query, matching the shape `updateStreaks()` already uses per-child and keeping the new helper a drop-in read-only sibling.
